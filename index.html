01 About

I didn't start with systems. I started by fixing small inconsistencies.

I design and lead with structure in mind — whether it's a product surface, a design system, or a team. I'm interested in how things hold together over time.

The past 7+ years have been spent inside complex product environments, mostly in project management tools. Leading design teams, owning systems, and making decisions that don't just solve for now — they reduce future friction.

Good design, to me, isn't just clarity on the surface. It's stability underneath.
